Deep, executive-level tracks on applying data and AI across the beverage business — beer, wine, whiskey, mead and the fast-growing non-alcoholic segment — from the production floor to the boardroom. Pick a track:

- **[Commercial Planning Analytics]({{ '/tracks/commercial-planning/' | relative_url }})** — portfolio, pricing, promo, S&OP, route-to-market, and lager sales blueprints by channel.
- **[Sales Intelligence]({{ '/tracks/sales-intelligence/' | relative_url }})** — depletions, account scoring, distributor management, churn, and channel sales blueprints (distributor, off- and on-premise, national accounts, e-commerce).
- **[Marketing]({{ '/tracks/marketing/' | relative_url }})** — segmentation, mix modeling, attribution, and the sober-curious consumer.
- **[Financial Planning & Analytics]({{ '/tracks/financial-planning-analytics/' | relative_url }})** — COGS, driver-based forecasting, margin bridges, cost-to-serve, and co-packing economics.
- **[ESG & Sustainability]({{ '/tracks/esg/' | relative_url }})** — energy, water, carbon (Scope 1/2/3), packaging, circular economy, GenAI reporting, and the UK, EU, US and India rules.
- **[EHS]({{ '/tracks/ehs/' | relative_url }})** — predictive safety, hazard mapping, compliance, and food safety.
- **[Sales Forecasting]({{ '/tracks/sales-forecasting/' | relative_url }})** — maturity model, seasonality, promo lift, accuracy, and honest limits.
- **[Brewing Science & AI]({{ '/tracks/brewing-science-ai/' | relative_url }})** — malt, water, hops, wort, yeast, fermentation, packaging and QC, plus Excel brewing calculators, IoT on the line, and contract-brewing analytics.
- **[Distilling & Maturation]({{ '/tracks/distilling-maturation/' | relative_url }})** — cut points, new-make spirit, the angel's share, cask selection, blending, bottling maturity, and warehouse IoT.
- **[Winemaking & AI]({{ '/tracks/winemaking-ai/' | relative_url }})** — grape ripeness and harvest timing, vineyard yield, fermentation, wine faults, blending, and precision viticulture.

**The cross-cutting toolkit** — every track draws on the same building blocks, applied to beer, whiskey and wine: data platforms (**[Microsoft Fabric]({{ '/2026/microsoft-fabric-for-breweries-20-use-cases/' | relative_url }})**, **[Databricks]({{ '/2026/databricks-for-breweries-20-use-cases/' | relative_url }})**, **[Snowflake]({{ '/2026/snowflake-for-breweries-20-use-cases/' | relative_url }})**), the **[Claude & ChatGPT]({{ '/2026/claude-ai-claude-code-for-breweries/' | relative_url }})** ecosystem, **[IoT]({{ '/2026/iot-in-the-brewery-sensors-process/' | relative_url }})** sensors, **[AI-adoption roadmaps]({{ '/2026/how-a-brewery-starts-with-ai-and-gen-ai/' | relative_url }})**, and **[Excel calculators]({{ '/2026/advanced-brewing-calculations-excel/' | relative_url }})** for the brewhouse.
