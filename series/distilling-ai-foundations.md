Everyone in the distilling industry keeps hearing "AI", "machine learning" and "generative AI" — usually from someone trying to sell something. This 5-part series gives you a straight, working definition of each, in plain language, with distillery examples and no sales deck. The aim isn't to impress you with the technology; it's to make you fluent enough to ask the one question that matters: *which of these do you actually mean, and does my problem even need it?*

No hype, as always. Where these tools earn their place in a distillery, I'll show you. Where they confidently lie, I'll show you that too.

## The series

1. **[What Is AI, Really? A Distiller's Plain-Language Guide]({{ '/2026/what-is-ai-distilling-plain-language/' | relative_url }})** — why "AI" is an umbrella over three different tools, and the one question that cuts through the hype.
2. **[What Is Machine Learning? Pattern-Finding for the Distillery]({{ '/2026/what-is-machine-learning-distillery/' | relative_url }})** — learning a pattern from your own records instead of writing a rule, and why a model is only ever a mirror of its data.
3. **[What Is Generative AI? The Difference That Matters for Distillers]({{ '/2026/what-is-generative-ai-distillers/' | relative_url }})** — the chatbot kind: brilliant at words, unreliable at numbers, and why grounding fixes most of it.
4. **[AI vs Machine Learning vs Generative AI: Which Is Which in the Distillery]({{ '/2026/ai-vs-machine-learning-vs-generative-ai-distillery/' | relative_url }})** — a decision test that routes any distillery problem to the right tool by the shape of the answer.
5. **[Where a Distillery Actually Starts with AI]({{ '/2026/where-a-distillery-starts-with-ai/' | relative_url }})** — not with a tool, but with one measured record — and the order of operations that makes all three pay off.

Read them in order — each builds on the last. Every post in the series is tagged [#distilling-ai-foundations]({{ '/tags/' | relative_url }}#distilling-ai-foundations).

When you're ready to go deeper into the spirits side, the full [Distilling &amp; Maturation track]({{ '/tracks/distilling-maturation/' | relative_url }}) covers cut points, the angel's share, cask selection, blending and bottling maturity — the practical AI work that builds on these foundations.
