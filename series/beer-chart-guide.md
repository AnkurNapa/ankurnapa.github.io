A practical field guide to data visualization for the drinks industry: **one article per chart type**, each with two or three concrete beer-industry use cases, so you can match the chart to the question instead of defaulting to whatever your tool drew first. No hype — where a chart is the wrong choice, I say so.

## The charts, one article each

**Comparison & composition**
1. **[Bar Chart]({{ '/2020/bar-chart-beer-use-cases/' | relative_url }})** — comparing values across categories (styles, SKUs, accounts).
2. **[Grouped (Clustered) Bar Chart]({{ '/2020/grouped-bar-chart-beer-use-cases/' | relative_url }})** — comparing several series side by side (this year vs last, plan vs actual).
3. **[Stacked Bar Chart]({{ '/2020/stacked-bar-chart-beer-use-cases/' | relative_url }})** — composition within each category (channel mix, cost build-up).
4. **[Pie &amp; Donut Chart]({{ '/2020/pie-donut-chart-beer-use-cases/' | relative_url }})** — share of a single whole, used sparingly.
5. **[Treemap]({{ '/2020/treemap-chart-beer-use-cases/' | relative_url }})** — composition with many parts and a hierarchy.

**Trends over time**
6. **[Line Chart]({{ '/2020/line-chart-beer-use-cases/' | relative_url }})** — trends and trajectories over time.
7. **[Combo (Line + Bar) Chart]({{ '/2020/combo-line-bar-chart-beer-use-cases/' | relative_url }})** — two metrics on two axes (volume + margin %).
8. **[Area &amp; Stacked Area Chart]({{ '/2020/area-chart-beer-use-cases/' | relative_url }})** — cumulative totals and shifting composition over time.

**Relationships & distributions**
9. **[Scatter Plot]({{ '/2020/scatter-plot-beer-use-cases/' | relative_url }})** — relationship between two variables.
10. **[Bubble Chart]({{ '/2020/bubble-chart-beer-use-cases/' | relative_url }})** — three variables at once.
11. **[Histogram]({{ '/2020/histogram-beer-use-cases/' | relative_url }})** — the distribution of one variable.
12. **[Box &amp; Whisker Plot]({{ '/2020/box-plot-beer-use-cases/' | relative_url }})** — spread, medians and outliers across groups.

**Two dimensions at once**
13. **[Heat Map]({{ '/2020/heat-map-beer-use-cases/' | relative_url }})** — intensity across two categories (hour × day, style × month).

**Specialized & operational**
14. **[Waterfall (Bridge) Chart]({{ '/2020/waterfall-chart-beer-use-cases/' | relative_url }})** — how a total changed (margin bridge, volume bridge, variance).
15. **[Funnel Chart]({{ '/2020/funnel-chart-beer-use-cases/' | relative_url }})** — stage-to-stage drop-off (sales pipeline, NPD stage-gate).
16. **[Gauge &amp; KPI Card]({{ '/2020/gauge-kpi-card-beer-use-cases/' | relative_url }})** — single metric vs target at a glance (production, OEE, OTIF).
17. **[Gantt Chart]({{ '/2020/gantt-chart-beer-use-cases/' | relative_url }})** — schedules and overlaps (tank occupancy, brew schedule, NPD timeline).
18. **[Geographic Map]({{ '/2020/geographic-map-beer-use-cases/' | relative_url }})** — when location is the variable (territory, coverage, catchment).
