You don't need to be a developer to build the small, specific tool your brewery actually needs — a batch logger, an IBU calculator, a fermentation tracker. With Claude's latest model, **Fable 5**, in Claude Code, a brewer can describe a tool in plain language and supervise the build. This short set shows how, honestly: the loop, what the new model changes, and an actual build log including the bug it introduced and how a known-answer check caught it.

No hype, as always. The model does the typing; your brewer's knowledge of what the numbers *should* be does the part that counts.
